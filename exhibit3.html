Zermatt and Matterhorn in the Morning

This year I spent my winter holidays with my family at Zermatt ski resort. Zermatt is a small picturesque town located at the foot of the Matterhorn. The entire town is a car-free zone. Almost all vehicles in Zermatt are battery driven and completely silent. There are number of places with the amazing view of Zermatt and Matterhorn, but most of these places are rather difficult to access. To reach this stunning view point I had to hike up the ski slope in the dead of the night for 20 minutes.

  • Photographer: Andrey Omelyanchuk
  • Location: Matterhorn, Switzerland

What Goes Up Must Come Down

The secret with Bhool Bhulaiya is quite intriguing actually. The fort, located in Lucknow, was so intricately planned that you'll be mesmerized by it's secrets when walking through its narrow corridors. The idea behind this fort/mahal was that when enemies attacked, they would easily get lost in its thin corridors. One would think that you must keep traveling downstairs to escape when the secret is to travel up to make your way down! This way, enemies were stuck inside with no way out.

  • Photographer: Nidhi Singh
  • Location: Lucknow, India
  • Category: Sense of Place
  • Date: July 2014
